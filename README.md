# Junior Business Analyst

## Professional Profile
Detail-oriented Junior Business Analyst with practical experience in remote environments, specializing in technical requirements elicitation and workflow optimization. Skilled in bridging the gap between business stakeholders and development teams by creating structured documentation, analyzing complex system architecture, and defining functional requirements. Proactive in applying data analysis and structured project management methodologies to deliver high-quality product solutions.

## Key Skills
* **Requirements Engineering:** Elicitation, User Stories, Acceptance Criteria, Functional Requirements, and Technical Documentation.
* **Technical & Architecture:** SQL (Complex Query Writing), 3-Tier Architecture Mapping (Presentation, Logic, Data layers), and Data Schema Analysis.
* **Modeling & Databases:** Process Mapping, ER Diagrams, Relational Databases (PostgreSQL, MS SQL), and Data Integration.
* **Project Management & Tools:** Advanced Jira Setup (Custom Boards, Backlog Management), Confluence, Agile/Scrum, and Target Audience Verification.
* **Business Strategy:** Market Analysis, Competitor Benchmarking, Advertising Strategy, and User Interview Modeling.
* **UX/UI Fundamentals:** Wireframing, Mockup Specifications, and Usability Requirements Definition.
